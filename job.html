AI-enabled Integrated Job Portal for Teachers

Empowering Teachers Across India

We are developing a Teacher Empowerment Portal to help educators register and create digital profiles that track their experience, qualifications, and feedback from schools and parents. Our platform allows teachers to build a verified digital identity and gain visibility across the nation without needing to migrate to cities for job opportunities.

Key Features

Unique Digital Profiles for Teachers

Teachers receive a unique ID similar to Aadhaar, enabling them to showcase their qualifications and experience to schools across India. With police verification and continuous training, their profiles provide a transparent view of their capabilities.

Rural Access to Quality Teachers

Schools in small towns and rural areas can now easily access trained and verified teachers, reducing the need for migration to urban areas. This helps bridge the gap between rural schools and qualified educators.

Performance-Linked Incentives

Teachers' promotions and incentives are linked directly to student outcomes, feedback, and satisfaction, creating a transparent and merit-based system for professional growth.

Seamless Onboarding

Our platform ensures real-time job allocation, continuous training cycles, and immediate feedback loops for teachers, ensuring they are always improving and ready to meet educational demands.

Transparency and Direct Access

The platform eliminates middlemen, providing direct job access to teachers. This creates an open, transparent system where schools and educators can connect without barriers.
