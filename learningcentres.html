About मातृकुलम् Learning Centers

Maatrkulam Learning Center is a network of 21st-century tech-enabled modern Gurukuls. We focus on empowering मातृशक्ति (women empowerment), मातृभाषा (native language), and मातृभूमि (our homeland).

Career-Oriented and Skill-Based Education

The focus on career-oriented learning from an early stage, coupled with the increasing importance of practical, hands-on skills, makes Maatrkulam Learning Centers attractive to parents seeking alternatives to the traditional education system. Our programs are designed to nurture not only academic success but also real-world skills, preparing students for future career opportunities.

Hybrid Learning Models

Our hybrid learning models combine the best aspects of homeschooling and traditional schooling through micro learning centers or group classes. These flexible programs offer families the option to balance personalized learning with social interaction, fostering both independence and collaboration among students.

Rural and Semi-Urban Growth

With increasing digital penetration, families in semi-urban and rural areas are also beginning to adopt homeschooling for its affordability and accessibility. Maatrkulam Learning Centers provide the infrastructure and support to make high-quality education accessible to all communities, regardless of geographical location.

Extended DayCare Program (EDP)

The Extended DayCare Program (EDP) is a 3-hour play-based Social-Emotional Learning (SEL) initiative. It offers a structured, screen-free environment for children's holistic development and real-world exploration.

Program Highlights

  • Scientific Curriculum Design: Our curriculum covers five core dimensions of child development.
  • Holistic Growth: Focus on cognitive, physical, social, and emotional growth, nurturing your child like a mother.

Foundational Program (Class 3 to 7)

Our Foundational Program is designed for students from Class 3 to Class 7, covering all essential domains of child development. We focus on building a strong foundation through:

  • Cognitive Development (I.Q.): Focused on enhancing intellectual abilities through interactive and problem-solving activities.
  • Psychomotor Development (Hand): Activities designed to improve motor skills and coordination through hands-on learning experiences.
  • Affective Development (E.Q.): Emotional intelligence education to help children understand and manage their emotions effectively.

Senior Program (Class 8 to 12)

With the shift towards competency-based learning as per CBSE guidelines, our Senior Program is crafted with a career-oriented approach. The composite score from Class 9 to Class 12 determines the overall percentage, aligning with modern educational standards.

The Senior Program follows the NIOS (National Institute of Open Schooling) model, offering flexibility and skill-based learning for students from Class 8 to Class 12. This prepares them for future career opportunities and helps them develop a comprehensive understanding of their subjects.

Our Curriculum Focus Areas

  • Cognitive Development: Structured learning activities to enhance problem-solving skills.
  • Language Development: Advanced language training through our Language Labs.
  • Physical Development: Indoor and outdoor games for physical fitness.
  • Aesthetic Development: Art-integrated learning including Art, Craft, Dance, and Music.
  • Social Development: Field trips and community visits to foster social connections.
  • Emotional Development: Parent-child interactions to strengthen bonds and instill core values.
